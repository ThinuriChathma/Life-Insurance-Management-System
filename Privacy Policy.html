Privacy And Policy



Last update : 30.04.2024


Introduction and Overview


Welcome to Safeguard Company's Privacy Policy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you interact with us online, including through our website, mobile applications, and other digital platforms. We understand the importance of safeguarding your privacy and are committed to ensuring the security and confidentiality of your data.


Our Commitment to Privacy


At Safeguard Company, we value your trust and are dedicated to protecting your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy explains:

  •           --What information we collect from you and how we collect it.
  •           --How we use the information we collect to provide and customize our insurance services.
  •           --When and with whom we share your information.
  •           --Your rights and choices regarding your personal data.
  •           --How we safeguard your information and maintain data security.
  •           --How you can contact us with questions or concerns about your privacy.

Information We Collect


We collect various types of information to process your insurance applications, customize insurance plans, and improve our services. This may include:

  •           --Personal information (e.g., name, address, contact details, date of birth).
  •           --Financial information (e.g., payment details, credit history).
  •           --Health and medical information (e.g., medical history, lifestyle habits) necessary for underwriting and customization of insurance policies.
  •           --Information about your interactions with us (e.g., website usage data, communication preferences).

How We Use Your Information


We use the information we collect for purposes such as:

  •           --Processing insurance applications and claims.
  •           --Customizing insurance plans to meet your needs.
  •           --Communicating with you about your policy, updates, and promotions.
  •           --Improving our products and services.
  •           --Preventing fraud and ensuring compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Security and Confidentiality


We employ robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include encryption, access controls, regular audits, and staff training on data protection best practices.


Your Privacy Rights


You have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its processing. You can also choose your communication preferences and opt-out of certain data uses. For more information on exercising your rights, please refer to the "Your Choices" section of this policy.


Updates to This Privacy Policy


We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our data practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes and seek your consent if required by law.


Contact Us


If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
